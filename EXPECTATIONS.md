# Expectations

This isn't a rulebook. It's what we expect from each other as people who respect each other's time.

---

**Honor your commitments.**
If you say you'll deliver something by Tuesday, deliver it by Tuesday. Life happens — that's fine. But say something early. A quick message like *"I'm stuck, I'll need until Thursday"* is always better than silence. Other people may be waiting on your work.

**Check your own work before sharing.**
Send work you've verified yourself. For code, that means it compiles and passes the H2 tests (`mvn test -DenableH2Tests=true`). For text, that means you've re-read it at least once. Nobody expects perfection — we do expect that you've done a basic check before handing it off.

**Speak up early.**
If something is blocked, confusing, or going in the wrong direction — say so. A short comment on the PR, a message in Discord, or a note on the issue is enough. Early feedback saves everyone time.

---

We're building something together. These expectations protect everyone's time, including yours.
